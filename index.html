Introducing Academy

About Us

Complementing the Consulting arm’s strategy offering, Academy builds technology capability to accelerate social good organisations.

We equip our partners in the social sector with sustainable technology solutions and knowledge to improve operational processes while minimising the need for technical knowledge.

What exactly are we doing?

We leverage free and open source platforms such as the Google and Github ecosystems to create easily implementable tech solutions.

Apart from being free, open-source, and instantly deployable, our products enable data analytics by integrating google cloud n drive features into the products.

Our Ongoing Work

Our recent products are mainly focused on volunteer management, team management and impact assessment.

Some examples of our products include: event management pages, volunteer management systems, bespoke organisation pages

Fun Fact: our home and project pages are also prototypes!
